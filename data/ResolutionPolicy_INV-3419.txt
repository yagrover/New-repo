Resolution Policy
Policy Title: Dispute Resolution Policy
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2023-01-01
Types of Disputes Covered:
- Pricing discrepancies
- Delivery issues
- Quality concerns
Steps for Resolution:
1. Submit a dispute form
2. Initial review by customer service
3. Escalation to management if unresolved
4. Final resolution within 30 days
Timeframes:
- Initial review: 7 days
- Escalation: 14 days
- Final resolution: 30 days
Documentation Required:
- Invoice
- PO
- Goods Receipt
- Contract Terms
